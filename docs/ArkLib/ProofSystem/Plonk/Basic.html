    We aim to formalize the Plonk protocol (as a polynomial IOP) as stated in the original Plonk paper.

    As we do so, we will break down Plonk into modular components, such as . We also plan to formalize various extensions of Plonk, such as Maller's optimization trick (which can be seen as a change on the PIOP level), integration with lookup arguments (i.e. logup), high-degree constraints, and more.

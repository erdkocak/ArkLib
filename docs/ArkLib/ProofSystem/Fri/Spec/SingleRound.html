    The FRI protocol #

    We describe the FRI oracle reduction as a composition of many single rounds, and a final (zero-interaction) query round where the oracle verifier makes all queries to all received oracle codewords.

    This formalisation tries to encompass all of the generalisations of the FRI low-degree test covered in the "A summary on the {FRI} low degree test" paper by Ulrich Haböck (Cryptology ePrint Archive, Paper 2022/1216), see: https://eprint.iacr.org/2022/1216

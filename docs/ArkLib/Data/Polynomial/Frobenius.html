    theorem Polynomial.frobenius_identity_in_ground_field {Fq : Type u_1} [Field Fq] [Fintype Fq] {h_Fq_char_prime : Fact (Nat.Prime (ringChar Fq))} (f g : Polynomial Fq) :
    (f + g) ^ Fintype.card Fq = f ^ Fintype.card Fq + g ^ Fintype.card Fq

    The Frobenius identity for polynomials in Fq[X]. The q-th power of a sum of polynomials is the sum of their q-th powers.

    The different ideal #

    Main definition #

    • Submodule.traceDual: The dual L-sub B-module under the trace form.
    • FractionalIdeal.dual: The dual fractional ideal under the trace form.
    • differentIdeal: The different ideal of an extension of integral domains.

    Main results #

    • conductor_mul_differentIdeal: If L = K[x], with x integral over A, then ùî£ * ùîá = (f'(x)) with f being the minimal polynomial of x.

    • not_dvd_differentIdeal_iff: A prime does not divide the different ideal iff it is unramified (in the sense of Algebra.IsUnramifiedAt).
